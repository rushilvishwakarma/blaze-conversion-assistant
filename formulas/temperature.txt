# Temperature Conversion Formula

Temperature conversions require specific formulas for each conversion rather than a simple multiplication factor. All temperatures can be converted by first converting to Celsius as an intermediate unit.

## Conversion Formulas:

### From any unit to Celsius (intermediate step):
- Celsius to Celsius: No change
- Fahrenheit to Celsius: (°F - 32) × 5/9
- Kelvin to Celsius: K - 273.15
- Rankine to Celsius: (°R - 491.67) × 5/9
- Reaumur to Celsius: °Re × 5/4

### From Celsius to target unit:
- Celsius to Celsius: No change
- Celsius to Fahrenheit: (°C × 9/5) + 32
- Celsius to Kelvin: °C + 273.15
- Celsius to Rankine: (°C × 9/5) + 491.67
- Celsius to Reaumur: °C × 4/5

## Examples:

1. Convert 68°F to Celsius:
   - (68 - 32) × 5/9 = 20°C

2. Convert 25°C to Kelvin:
   - 25 + 273.15 = 298.15K

3. Convert 300K to Fahrenheit:
   - First to Celsius: 300 - 273.15 = 26.85°C
   - Then to Fahrenheit: 26.85 × 9/5 + 32 = 80.33°F

---

Use these formulas for all temperature conversions.

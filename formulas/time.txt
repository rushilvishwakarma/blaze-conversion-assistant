# Time Conversion Formula

To convert between any two time units, first convert the input value to seconds, then from seconds to the target unit.

## Conversion to seconds (base unit):

- Years (average): 1 year = 31,557,600 seconds (365.25 days)
- Months (average): 1 month = 2,629,800 seconds (30.44 days)
- Weeks: 1 week = 604,800 seconds
- Days: 1 day = 86,400 seconds
- Hours: 1 hour = 3,600 seconds
- Minutes: 1 minute = 60 seconds
- Seconds: 1 second = 1 second
- Milliseconds: 1 millisecond = 0.001 seconds
- Microseconds: 1 microsecond = 0.000001 seconds
- Nanoseconds: 1 nanosecond = 1e-9 seconds
- Picoseconds: 1 picosecond = 1e-12 seconds

## General Formula

1. Convert input value to seconds:
   seconds = value * (conversion factor of fromUnit)
2. Convert seconds to target unit:
   result = seconds / (conversion factor of toUnit)

## Example
Convert 3 days to hours:
- 3 days * 86,400 = 259,200 seconds   
- 259,200 / 3,600 = 72 hours

---

Use this formula for all time conversions.
